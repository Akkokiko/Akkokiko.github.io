The Giants Causeway
Maciej Polubiec

The Giants Causeway is a very unique area on the north coast of Northern Ireland. Its major feature is a special building. The Giants Causeway is made of about 40.000 basalt columns. Most of them have a shape of a hexagon. The whole composition makes a wonderful and impressing view.

If you want to visit the Giants Causeway, you should choose a proper route.

The Visitor Centren

The Visitor Center is some kind of museum. You can find there a lot of exhibitions about history, mythology and the building of Giants Causeway. I recommend you to visit this place. It should change your point of view while walking.
